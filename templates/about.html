Designed for students, by students

Available for everyone, contributable by anyone, let's improve learning together.

Say goodbye to the frustration of not knowing what to study.

This website lets users to create cheat sheet for any subject or domain in order for knowledge to be available to the public.
Thanks to all the cheat sheet in the CSM (Cheat Sheet Market), people will better study and learn.
This project is open-source, as it is available on GitHub .

Cheat sheet are short files in which only the essential points are written.
It helps users remember and sharpen their understanding on a certain topic, either by making one helping their review but also other people, or by finding one that corresponds to the user's expectations around a subject.
