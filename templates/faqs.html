Frequently asked questions

Newlines are not rendered correctly in cheat sheet, why?

That is because the cheat sheet does not render its content raw. Before any newline, you have to add a backslash character for it to work (due to the way the module zero-md renders newlines).
In case you want to do newlines in your $\LaTeX$ formula, you need to put three backslashes instead of two. 

                \begin{cases}
                    y=2x-4\\\
                    7y-9x+9=0
                \end{cases}
This will render in a cheat sheet as $\begin{cases}y=2x-4\\7y-9x+9=0\end{cases}$
